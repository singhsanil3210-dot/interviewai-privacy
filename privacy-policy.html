Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

InterviewAI ("we", "our", or "us") is a Chrome extension that provides real-time AI-powered answer suggestions to help users practice and prepare for interviews. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information solely to provide our service:

  • Audio data: We capture tab audio to transcribe spoken interview questions in real time using Deepgram's transcription API.
  • Transcript text: The transcribed text of interview questions is sent to our AI backend to generate answer suggestions.
  • User preferences: We store your role/context input locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only to:

  • Transcribe interview questions in real time
  • Generate AI-powered answer suggestions
  • Save your preferences locally on your device

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing interview assistance.

4. Data Sharing

We share limited data with the following third-party services solely to operate the extension:

  • Deepgram: Audio data is sent to Deepgram for speech-to-text transcription.
  • OpenAI: Transcribed text is sent to OpenAI to generate answer suggestions.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any other third parties.

5. Data Storage

We do not store audio recordings or transcripts on our servers. Audio is processed in real time and immediately discarded. User preferences are stored locally on your device only.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any audio or transcript data after your session ends. Local preferences stored on your device can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the extension at any time
  • Clear locally stored preferences by uninstalling the extension
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

8. Security

All data transmitted to third-party services is sent over secure HTTPS connections. We do not store sensitive data on our servers.

9. Children's Privacy

InterviewAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
